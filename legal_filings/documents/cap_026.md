to distribute and dispense controlled substances and aiding and assisting the
preparation of a false and fraudulent tax return. He raises several claims of
ineffective assistance of trial counsel, which we decline to review on direct
appeal. See United States v. Isgar, 739 F.3d 829, 841 (5th Cir. 2014). He also
