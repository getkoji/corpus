***NOTE 1 - ORGANIZATION AND BASIS OF PRESENTATION***

Naploy Corp. was incorporated in the State of Wyoming

and established on April 6, 2023. Our primary focus is on launching a news blog that provides the latest updates on health-related topics,

including but not limited to medical breakthroughs, healthy lifestyle tips, and updates on healthcare policies.

***NOTE 2 - GOING CONCERN***

The Company's financial statements have been

prepared assuming that it will continue as a going concern, which contemplates continuity of operations, realization of assets, and liquidation

of liabilities in the normal course of business.

As reflected in the financial statements, the Company

had an accumulated deficit of $ 56,179 at April 30, 2026, a net loss of $ 8,784 for the nine months ended April 30, 2026. These factors

raise substantial doubt about the Company's ability to continue as a going concern.
