We represent the above-referenced insured with respect to damages sustained on 08/01/2026. We are writing to advise you of our subrogation claim and to demand reimbursement for payments made under our policy.

- Damaged drywall and paint in main office and reception area

- Damaged personal property and furnishings
- Mold remediation required in affected areas
